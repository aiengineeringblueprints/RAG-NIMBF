\section{Discussion}

The benchmark results should be interpreted in relation to the target use case. High retrieval recall may increase context size and cost, while stronger reranking may improve evidence quality at the expense of latency. Prompt templates can shift the balance between concise answers and detailed grounded explanations.

Threats to validity include dataset size, evaluator reliability, domain mismatch, stochastic model behavior, and changes in external model providers. Repeated runs and fixed experiment artifacts help reduce these risks.
